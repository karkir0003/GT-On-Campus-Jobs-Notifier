About

Helping GT students land on-campus jobs

Most of the on-campus jobs at Georgia Tech are posted on here. Vacancies are filled fast, and there is no way to get notified as soon as a new vacancy is posted. Hence, we built our tool, which sends users an email notification as soon as a new job is posted on the website. We hope you will subscribe, and this web app will help you land your next on-campus job.

We are always working to optimize and add new features. Current version: 1.0
